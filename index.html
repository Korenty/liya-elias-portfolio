The Origin

October 29 — Saint Gabriel's Child.

Born on October 29 (ጥቅምት 19), Liya embodies the protective and monumental spirit of her namesake. Her existence is a Scorpionic masterclass — unwavering loyalty, magnetic depth, and a transformative vision that turns every project into a legacy.

Guided by the moral compass of her parents, Elias Negede and Birhane Haile, and sharing her path with her twin soul Lidya Elias, family is her ultimate blueprint. She graduated with a BSc in Architecture with Honours from Hope Enterprises University College in 2021.
